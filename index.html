Our Mission

The Manchuro Climate Accord aims to unite micronations and micronational organizations worldwide in the fight against climate change through education, advocacy, and collaborative action.

Global Unity

Bringing together micronations and organizations from across the globe to form a united front against climate change.

Education

Educating citizens and leaders about climate science, sustainable practices, and effective climate policies.

Grassroots Action

Empowering local communities to implement climate solutions that can scale to regional, national, and international levels.
